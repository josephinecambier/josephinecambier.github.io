Het Nationale Theater

Refreshed brand identity for the dutch leading institution for theatre programming and new theater productions

Het Nationale Theater

Brand identity | 2019

hnt.nl


In 2017, Het Nationale Theater was born from the fusion of the national theater company and the most influential theater halls from The Hague. The identity was created during the merger, definitely under pressure.

After only 18month it was time for a solid brand strategy and a serious refreshment of the visual identity. The challenge was to develop a very iconic brand under which every department can gather but also find the freedom to address their unique challenges. Kid plays during the holidays, theater making workshop for teenagers, avant-garde cutting-edges experimental theater pieces, popular stand-uppers, family shows, theater classics, dance, music, festivals and parties: Het Nationale Theater has it all. But how can they speak from one voice? They all believe that Theater can change the world.

Melkweg

Brand identity and website for one of the most popular music venue in Amsterdam

Melkweg

Branding | 2016
And more since: branding, event, campaign, etc.

melkweg.nl


In 2016 we were challenged to create a new visual identity for the Melkweg. This also meant refreshing the website created by Grrr in 2013.

Melkweg is staging all styles of music between pop and alternative cultures. It is this diversity that defines Melkweg, but this was also the challenge of the project: How to create a visual language that would appeal to a broad array of subcultures?

Our solution was to develop a font for Melkweg; a font that would become the imagery. The idea is simple: The E turns into an M and the M turns into a W. This resulted in a very flexible language with a raw and DIY mentality.

Yoni

Website and brand implementation for a femcare brand breaking taboos

Yoni

Website | 2017

yoni.care


Yoni is a brand of sustainable tampons, pads and panty liners.

Yoni started from an activist motivation, “Chemicals are not for Pussies”, and turned into a sustainable brand with a rather fashionable and quirky tone of voice. But the branding (by Thonik) consciously doesn’t show that. It plays on a high-quality and transparent appearance, almost clinical.

The challenge was to bring those two worlds together and find the adequate look and feel: Exciting and trustworthy, fashionable and transparent.

Website | Constantly iterating since 2016

BinnensteBuiten

Refreshed brand identity and Website | 2019

https://binnenstebuiten.kro-ncrv.nl/


BinnensteBuiten is a slow pace TV show giving daily inspiration for cooking, gardening, walks, home design, diy. All things you can do in- and outside the house.

BinnensteBuiten wanted to reach a younger audience and had for ambition to become a rich online inspiration magazine with unique content. We refreshed the brand identity to a more today sustainable lifestyle brand for everyone, with warm and vintage typefaces and colors.

Winq

Art direction for online gay lifestyle magazine

Winq

Website | 2017

winq.nl


Winq is a premium quarterly magazine for gay men. The goal was to align their online channels to the visually high standards of Winq magazine. Furthermore Winq was in need of a strategic advisor to bring the low key gay.nl and the premium quality Winq brand together as one.

We introduced a new way to present content. Instead of a chronological overview we created themes and categories so the editors can curate the website as well as they curate their magazine. We also helped them in their editorial change toward more long-reads with features such as a progress bar, cheers, and “read later”.

The art direction was directly inspired from the paper magazine: a simple, elegant, and masculine black and white set-up.
