Yoni

Yoni

Website | 2017

yoni.care


Yoni is a brand of sustainable tampons, pads and panty liners. Yoni started from an activist motivation, “Chemicals are not for Pussies”, and turned into a sustainable brand with a rather fashionable and quirky tone of voice. But the branding (by Thonik) consciously doesn’t show that. It plays on a high-quality and transparent appearance, almost clinical.

The challenge was to bring those two worlds together and find the adequate Look & Feel: Exciting and trustworthy, fashionable and transparent.


Role: Art Director & Designer
